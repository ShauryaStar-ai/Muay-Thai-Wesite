1. Stance and Guard

    Feet shoulder-width apart
    Lead foot slightly forward, back foot at an angle
    Hands up high (protecting chin), elbows in
    Chin tucked, eyes forward
    Slight bounce for mobility
            

        
2. Basic Strikes

    Punches (มวยหมัด / Muay Mat)
    Jab  fast, straight punch with lead hand
    Cross  powerful rear-hand punch
    Hook  curved punch to the side of the head/body
    Uppercut upward punch to the chin
    Horizontal elbow
    Upward elbow
    Spinning elbow
    Kicks (เตะ / Te)
    Roundhouse kick (most famous)
    Teep (push kick, like a front kick)
    Knees (เข่า / Khao)
    Straight knee
    Jumping knee
    Knee in clinch
